About Me

Hello, my name is Randall Phillips and I am in the process of becoming a full-stack web developer. My previous experience with coding prior to this Bootcamp is a couple of Java courses at UNC-C, but now I have my own website! I am very excited for my future in tech and web development, and soon I hope to be knowledgable enough to start working in the industry. My dream is to be able to create digital animations for either websites, games, or any number of markets with the need for high-quality graphical representation such as law-firms and law enforcement, hospitals and medical practices alike. I believe that the future is with computers, and a solid coding skill-set will be crucial for me to thrive.


I am currently 22-years old, and I live in the No Da neighborhood just a couple miles North of Uptown Charlotte. I am originally from Fort Mill, South Carolina, where I grew up from the age of nine until I graduated High School and left for Charleston, SC for my undergraduate degree. I played high-level soccer for my entire youth career before hanging up the boots for College. I still play on a weekly basis, usually in South Charlotte on Sunday's in a men's league, and it's a great way to meet some people in the area. My other hobbies include skateboarding whenever possible, watching soccer, and sketching tattoo ideas.
